## What to Skip Entirely

- Multiple links / linktree (one strong link > five weak ones, week 1)
- Action buttons like "Book now" / "Call now"
- Hours of operation
- Business-style "Our story" long-form
- Fake follower-count tricks
- Emoji-heavy bio (the honesty voice doesn't need emojis)
