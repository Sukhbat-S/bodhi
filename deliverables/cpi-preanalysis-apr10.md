## 3. The Pattern

**Soft CPI (below consensus)**: 2 out of 2 times, BTC rallied 2.1-2.6% within 1 hour. But the Nov 2025 print reversed intraday -- the initial pump got sold.

**Hot CPI (above consensus)**: 2 out of 2 times, BTC barely moved (-0.3% to +0.8%). The market has been shrugging off hot prints because it attributes them to supply shocks (oil), not demand overheating.

**In-line CPI**: Both times, +0.5-0.7%. Boring moves.

**Hit rate for "soft CPI = pump"**: 2/2 = 100% directionally, but magnitude fades. The Nov 2025 print gave back most gains same day.

**Key insight**: The asymmetry is bullish. Hot prints don't dump BTC hard (because the market knows it's energy, not demand). Soft prints pump BTC (because it signals rate cuts sooner). This creates a positive expected value for a long bias going into CPI.
