# UB Restaurant Facebook Targets — Agency Pitch Research
*April 9, 2026 | Compiled for Sukhbat+Odko business automation agency prospecting*
*Next target after Nagomi. Gaps found = the pitch.*

---

## 1. Modern Nomads
**Facebook:** facebook.com/modernnomads.mn | **Followers:** ~35K  
**Website:** modernnomads.mn | **Cuisine:** Traditional Mongolian, Asian fusion | **Locations:** 5+ (UB + airport + Darkhan)

**What they do well:** Active Reels section. QR menu system. Chain presence signals operational maturity. English menu available. One of the most-followed food pages in UB.

**Gaps:**
- No Messenger bot — reservations handled manually via DM
- No own ordering system — delivery only via Songo (third-party, 30% commission)
- No English-language posts — Mongolian-only content misses tourist/expat segment during summer peak
- Website exists but zero conversion optimization (no booking, no loyalty, no email capture)

**Pitch:** Messenger reservation bot + English content cadence for tourist season. ROI story: 5 locations × bot automation = meaningful staff hour savings.

## 2. Rosewood Kitchen + Enoteca
**Facebook:** facebook.com/RosewoodKitchenEnoteca | **Followers:** ~31K  
**Website:** rosewood-restaurant.com | **Cuisine:** Italian, Western | **Est:** 2011

**What they do well:** Professional website with full menus. Active on Reels and Instagram (@rosewoodkitchenub). 13-year brand, expat/tourist staple. English-first content.

**Gaps:**
- Instagram only 6.5K vs 31K Facebook — massive cross-channel gap, audience not converted
- No integrated online reservation system — Messenger DM only (manual)
- No own delivery — Songo only
- "On extended vacation" notice seen publicly — operational inconsistency signals brand risk

**Pitch:** Automated reservation widget + Instagram growth bridge. Harder sell (most mature digitally) but Instagram gap is a concrete number they can see.

## 3. Mongolian's Restaurant (Shangri-La Mall)
**Facebook:** facebook.com/mongolians2 | **Followers:** ~28K  
**Website:** None | **Cuisine:** Traditional Mongolian | **Est:** 2008

**What they do well:** Premium location (Shangri-La Mall, 200m from Sukhbaatar Square). Strong cultural identity — museum-like ambiance, horse intestine salad, steamed sheep's head. English-speaking staff. TripAdvisor 4.3/5, ranked #27 of 292 in UB.

**Gaps:**
- No dedicated website — only referenced via Shangri-La Mall's page
- No online ordering, no delivery
- No Messenger bot
- Menu not available in any findable online format
- English posts on Facebook absent or sparse
- Zero tourist capture despite prime tourist-facing location

**Pitch:** Full digital presence build — landing page, online menu, Messenger booking bot, English content calendar. Most gaps = biggest contract potential. Premium brand already exists, just invisible online.

## 4. Veranda
**Facebook:** facebook.com/VerandaOfficial | **Followers:** ~27.6K  
**Website:** veranda.mn | **Cuisine:** Mediterranean, Italian | **Est:** 2006

**What they do well:** Has own website. QR menu live. Listed on Songo delivery. Active Instagram. English-speaking staff. Described as "first Mediterranean restaurant in Mongolia" — clear positioning.

**Gaps:**
- QR menu hosted on third-party qrmenu.mn — not integrated, breaks the brand loop
- Messenger reservations are manual, no automation
- No direct online ordering — Songo only
- English posts unconfirmed; content strategy unclear
- veranda.mn not optimized for tourist discovery (SEO, booking, Google Maps sync)

**Pitch:** Messenger automation + integrated booking + English post cadence. Most "almost there" restaurant — quick wins, fast pitch.

## 5. Ikh Mongol
**Facebook:** facebook.com/IkhmongolRestaurant | **Followers:** ~17K  
**Website:** None | **Cuisine:** Traditional Mongolian | **Location:** City center

**What they do well:** Strong cultural brand. 438+ reviews, 88% recommendation rate. Solid Facebook presence. City center location. Capacity for 50-60 (main hall) — events potential.

**Gaps:**
- No website
- No online ordering
- No delivery integration
- No Messenger bot
- No QR menu
- Almost certainly Mongolian-only posts
- Below 20K threshold — growing but not yet major player

**Pitch:** Full digital foundation from scratch. Blank slate = largest transformation, best case study potential for agency portfolio.
