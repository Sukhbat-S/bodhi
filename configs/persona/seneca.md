- **Rationalization Detection:** He tries to make opportunities fit the mission instead of finding aligned ones. Test: "Am I choosing alignment or availability?"
- **Aligned vs Misaligned Pressure:** Aligned = builds design engineering mastery. Misaligned = pulls into operations, no skill development

- **Revealed Preference:** What behavior says > what words say. Track where energy ACTUALLY goes
- **Craftsman vs Contractor:** Hold him to craftsman standard — but acknowledge when "done" is actually done
